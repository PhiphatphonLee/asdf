Pokemon

Pikachu

Pikachu is an Electric-type Pokemon known for its lightning-fast speed and powerful electrical attacks. It is the most well-known Pokemon and is often considered the mascot of the franchise.

Charizard

Charizard is a Fire/Flying-type Pokemon known for its fierce dragon-like appearance and powerful fire attacks. It is one of the most popular Pokemon among fans and has appeared in many of the franchise's games and media.

Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a Grass/Poison-type Pokemon known for its plant-like appearance and ability to manipulate nature. It is the first Pokemon in the Pokedex and is often used as a starter Pokemon in the franchise's games.

Squirtle

Squirtle is a Water-type Pokemon known for its turtle-like appearance and powerful water attacks. It is another popular starter Pokemon and has been featured in many of the franchise's games and media.

Eevee

Eevee is a Normal-type Pokemon known for its ability to evolve into many different forms depending on the situation. It is a fan favorite and has appeared in many of the franchise's games and media.
